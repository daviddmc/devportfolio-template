About Me

I am currently a senior undergraduate student in Department of Engineering Physics, Tsinghua University.

Since Fall 2015, I have been working at Key Laboratory of Particle and Radiation Imaging (Tsinghua University), Ministry of Education, advised by Prof. Kui Ying. In Spring 2016, I took part in a Student Research Training Program under under supervision of Prof. Hua Guo in Advanced MRI Technique Laboratory. In Spring 2017, I work in Intelligent Vision Group, advised by Prof. Jianjiang Feng. In Summer 2017, I was fortunate enough to be selected into Stanford UGVR program and worked as a research assistant in Magnetic Resonance Systems Research Laboratory and Center for Advanced Functional Neuroimaging, advised by Prof. John Pauly and Prof. Greg Zaharchuk.

My research interests lie in medical imaging and deep learning, especially reconstruction and post-processing of PET and MRI.
